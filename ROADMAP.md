# Product Designer OS Roadmap

> The goal is not to finish this roadmap.
>
> The goal is to receive a product design offer.

---

**Objective**

Become interview-ready and build enough evidence to get a product design job.

## Mission 0

- Setup the system
- Resume
- LinkedIn
- Portfolio structure
- GitHub
- Daily routine
