# Philosophy

Traditional courses teach knowledge first.

```
Research

↓

Wireframes

↓

UI

↓

Portfolio
```

PDOS teaches the opposite.

```
Mission

↓

Knowledge

↓

Execution

↓

Case Study

↓

Portfolio

↓

Interview

↓

Offer Letter
```

You never learn something unless a mission requires it.

---

# Why only seven missions?

A world-class product designer is not someone who has completed 100 projects.

A world-class product designer has repeatedly demonstrated exceptional thinking across different kinds of problems.

These seven missions cover nearly every capability expected from a modern product designer.

---

# Mission 01

## Design a Consumer Product

### Goal

Learn the complete end-to-end product design process.

### You'll Learn

- User Research
- Psychology
- UX
- Interaction Design
- Visual Design
- Product Thinking
- Usability Testing

### Portfolio Outcome

One flagship case study demonstrating strong product fundamentals.
